Class Overview

witch is 1 of the 2 Intelligence characters in Poe2 and has a magic skill tree called Occult. The occult tree looks something like this.

This tree shares a trait with the other full int skill tree, and that is that none of their skills require a weapon to cast. Int casters are much more free to do what they please and, as a trade off, Don't scale damage as high or as easily as other classes.

Ascendancy Breakdown

Now I'll detail WHY Bloodmage is strong and what the weaknesses you have to watch out for.

Weaknesses I've currently gathered are:

  • You heavily rely on critting skills and making it consistent
  • Volatile health total, which leads to you not really knowing where your health is at a lot of the time.
  • Main Skill Cast times are really long.
  • Your build is heavy on spirit use. Might require high spirit on armor to optimize.

Those are the biggest ones. Another small point is the lack of mob clearing (If we dont add my secret sauce >:]). We'll cover that later. To stay on topic the list of strengths you get going into bloodmage and witch are:

  • High single target dps output that is very self sufficient.
  • Very High survivability while clearing
  • Very few "required" skills to make the build function
  • High amount of build origionality after main passive nodes.

As you can see there are "a lot" of positives. I love this build, and next I'll talk about why the single target damage gets so high. Trust me it is so silly.

Single Target Meltdown

The single target damage comes from literally 1 skill.

Bonestorm

Not kidding that's all you need. This skill hits a large number of times; this depends on the amount of time you put in to casting it. An interesting thing to note is after a certain amount of cast time (around 30%) it spans wide and stops getting more concetrated. This means that smaller targets will only ever need small amounts of the cast time to get hit with full value, BUT bosses and bigger hitboxes get absolutely shredded with a full blast. Last thing to note is each projectile hits seperately, and this is huge for statistically guaranteeing a blood orb on each possible cast.

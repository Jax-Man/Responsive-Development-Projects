Ascendancy Breakdown

Now I'll detail WHY Bloodmage is strong and what the weaknesses you have to watch out for.

Starting with weaknesses. In my experience they are:

  • Heavily relies on critting with skills and making it consistent
  • Aside from one skill. You get very close to enemies and will die to some attacks.
  • Bonestorm Cast times are really long.
  • Your build is heavy on spirit use. Requires high spirit on armor to optimize.
  • Clearing hordes will be hard.(without my secret sauce >:])

Strengths you get going into Bloodmage ascension and this build are:

  • High single target dps output that is also going to help you sustain your health.
  • Very High survivability to any hit that doesn't straight up kill you.
  • Very few "required" skills to make the build function.
  • High amount of build origionality. You can do a lot of your own stuff with this one

As you can see there are "a lot" of positives. I love this build, and next I'll talk about why the single target damage gets so high. Trust me it is so silly.

Single Target Meltdown

The single target damage comes from literally 1 skill.

Bonestorm

Not kidding that's all you need. This skill hits a large number of times; this depends on the amount of time you put in to casting it. An interesting thing to note is after a certain amount of cast time (around 30%) it doesn't get more concentrated it just gets wider. This means that smaller targets will need smaller amounts of the cast time to get hit with full value, BUT bosses and bigger hitboxes get absolutely shredded with a full cast. Last thing to note is each projectile hits seperately, and this is huge for statistically guaranteeing a blood orb on each possible cast.
